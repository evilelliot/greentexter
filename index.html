Greentexter is a simple tool that allows us to meme stories and visualise them in real time as if we were posting them on 4chan, with the advantage of not having to convert to white supremacism in the process.

How to use it?

  1. Come up with a funny title for your story and write it down.
  2. write your greentext, don't forget traditions such as 'being me'.
  3. click the download button and receive an image of your story.
